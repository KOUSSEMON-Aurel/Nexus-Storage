Nexus Media Archiver

A secure tool for personal media backup and encrypted metadata synchronization. Nexus helps users organize their private video content and maintain a database of their archives using Google Drive and YouTube APIs.

View on GitHub

Built for Absolute Persistence

Zero-Knowledge

Your files are encrypted with XChaCha20-Poly1305 before they even reach the server. Google only sees static noise.

Infrastructural Resilience

By utilizing robust cloud architectures, your media inherits reliable redundancy and high-availability.

Smart Indexing

A local SQLite FTS5 database manages your file tree and metadata, which is seamlessly synced via Google Drive.

Our Transparency Commitment

How we use Google Data

Nexus Media Archiver requests access to your Google Account to function safely:

  • • YouTube Data API: Used to upload your video content to private playlists on your account and to list them for organization. No existing videos are modified.
  • • Google Drive (File-level): Used only to store and sync the nexus.db index database, allowing seamless metadata synchronization across your devices.
App's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
