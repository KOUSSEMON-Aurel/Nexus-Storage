Built for Absolute Persistence

Zero-Knowledge

Your files are encrypted with XChaCha20-Poly1305 before they even reach the server. Google only sees static noise.

Infrastructural Resilience

By leveraging YouTube's 4K video infrastructure, your data inherits multi-region redundancy and high-availability.

Smart Indexing

A local SQLite FTS5 database manages your file tree and metadata, which is seamlessly synced via Google Drive.

Our Transparency Commitment

How we use Google Data

Nexus Storage requests access to two specific areas of your Google Account to function as a storage bridge:

  • • YouTube Data API: Used exclusively to upload encrypted video shards to private playlists on your account and to list them for recovery. No existing videos are modified.
  • • Google Drive (File-level): Used only to store and sync the nexus.db manifest, allowing you to access your archive from multiple desktop devices.
App's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
