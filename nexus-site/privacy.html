Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how Nexus Storage ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information from users ("you") when you use the Nexus Storage application.

1. Information Collection

Nexus Storage is a decentralized archival tool. We collect minimum information to function:

  • Google Account Identifiers: We use OAuth 2.0 to authenticate your session. We only receive your unique Google ID (`sub`) to derive your encryption key locally.
  • Application Data: We access your YouTube playlists and a specific file in your Google Drive (`nexus.db`) solely for storage and synchronization.

2. Use of Google User Data

We strictly use the information received from Google APIs for the following purposes:

  • YouTube Upload: To upload encrypted binary data sharded as video frames to your private playlists.
  • Google Drive Storage: To store and retrieve your decentralized file index (`nexus.db`) across your own devices.
Google API Limited Use Disclosure:
Nexus Storage's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

3. Information Sharing

Nexus Storage does not share your data with third parties. Your encryption keys are derived locally and never leave your device. Google only sees encrypted "static" video noise when you upload files.

4. Security

Your files are protected by XChaCha20-Poly1305 (256-bit) authenticated encryption. We employ zero-knowledge architecture, meaning we have no access to your decrypted content or passwords.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at our GitHub Repository.
